
A game about hitting an apple as far down the hall as possible while avoiding obstacles and the encroaching rot.

As apple moves along, fog color increases in a 1:1:4 rgb ratio.

Powerups
    Coins + Vending machine?
    Speed
    Jump height
    Spear power
    Spear cooldown
    Lighter apple

    Apple types (1 at a time):
        Antigrav apple
        Apple recall
        Ghost apple (pass through walls)
        Golden apple (drops coin when hit)
